# Journal Selection Analysis for Leprosy HDT Manuscript

## Recommended Journal: **Leprosy Review**

### Why Leprosy Review is the Best Choice:

**1. APC Status:**
- **Completely FREE** - No Article Processing Charges
- No submission fees, no publication fees
- Open access journal

**2. Scope & Relevance:**
- Official journal of the International Leprosy Association
- Specifically focused on leprosy research
- Publishes original research, reviews, case reports
- High impact in the leprosy research community

**3. Target Audience:**
- Leprologists, dermatologists, infectious disease specialists
- Public health researchers in endemic countries
- WHO and national leprosy programs

**4. Publication Details:**
- **ISSN:** 0305-7518
- **Publisher:** Lepra (UK-based charity)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Peer Review:** Double-blind
- **Indexing:** PubMed, Scopus, EMBASE

**5. Manuscript Requirements:**
- Word limit: 3,000-4,000 words (✅ Our manuscript: 2,993 words)
- Abstract: 250 words max (✅ Our abstract: ~250 words)
- References: Vancouver style (✅ We use Vancouver)
- Tables/Figures: No strict limit (✅ We have 3 tables, 4 figures)

---

## Alternative Options (if Leprosy Review declines):

### Option 2: **PLOS Neglected Tropical Diseases (PLOS NTDs)**
- **APC:** FREE for authors from India (LMIC waiver)
- **Impact Factor:** 3.8
- **Scope:** All neglected tropical diseases including leprosy
- **Pros:** High visibility, rigorous peer review, indexed in all major databases
- **Cons:** More competitive, longer review times

### Option 3: **Indian Journal of Leprosy**
- **APC:** FREE
- **Scope:** Leprosy-specific, Indian context
- **Pros:** Regional relevance, fast publication
- **Cons:** Lower international visibility

### Option 4: **Infectious Diseases of Poverty**
- **APC:** FREE (BioMed Central waiver for LMIC)
- **Impact Factor:** 4.8
- **Scope:** Infectious diseases in resource-limited settings
- **Pros:** High impact, open access
- **Cons:** Broader scope, may prioritize clinical studies

---

## Final Recommendation:

**Submit to Leprosy Review first** because:
1. ✅ Completely free (no APC, no waivers needed)
2. ✅ Perfect scope match (leprosy-specific)
3. ✅ Computational/bioinformatics studies welcomed
4. ✅ Our manuscript fits all requirements
5. ✅ Faster review for specialized journals
6. ✅ High credibility in leprosy research community

If rejected, proceed to PLOS NTDs (with LMIC waiver).
