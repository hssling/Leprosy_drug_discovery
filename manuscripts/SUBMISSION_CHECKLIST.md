## Submission Portal Information

**Journal:** Leprosy Review  
**Website:** https://leprosyreview.org  
**Submission System:** Editorial Manager (https://www.editorialmanager.com/leprev)

## Submission Steps

1. Create account on Editorial Manager
2. Select "Submit New Manuscript"
3. Choose article type: "Original Article"
4. Upload files in order:
   - Cover letter (PDF or DOCX)
   - Main manuscript (DOCX)
   - Figures (PNG/PDF - high resolution)
   - Supplementary files
5. Enter metadata:
   - Title
   - Abstract
   - Keywords
   - Author information
   - Suggested reviewers
6. Review and submit

## Post-Submission

- Expect acknowledgment within 2-3 days
- Initial editorial decision: 2-4 weeks
- Peer review: 4-8 weeks
- Revision (if needed): 2-4 weeks
- Final decision: 8-12 weeks total
